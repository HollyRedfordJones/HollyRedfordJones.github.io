About Holly

A singer-songwriter hailing from Chesterfield and growing up in the shadow of 'not quite being from Sheffield', Holly Redford Jones recently completed her university degree in politics, philosphy (and jazz) before embarking on a full time musical career - her long time passion. Holly is an experienced performer, a regular face on the Oxford circuit as well as performing further afield at festivals, balls, weddings and private events and, (a personal favourite), political rallies. Holly recieves plaudits as a singer-songwriter and jazz singer for her smokey range and powerfull voice influenced by Ella Fitzgerald and Nina Simone, she also electrifies in jazz-funk sets drawing on a talented circle of musical collaborators.

Holly is currently recording her debut EP in London at the Abbey Road Institute whilst continuing to work on new material. For more information or to book Holly please contact holly.redfordjones@gmail.com
