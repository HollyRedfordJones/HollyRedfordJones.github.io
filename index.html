About Holly

A singer-songwriter hailing from Chesterfield, Holly Redford Jones grew up in the shadow of not quite being from Sheffield. After years of hiding away in the embrace of Leonard Cohen, she found jazz, and moved on with healthy doses of Nina and Ella. She describes singing as therapeutic and addictive.
